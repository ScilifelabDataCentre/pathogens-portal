Data available from research groups in the Netherlands

The list of available data on this Portal was curated manually until July 2023. We only include data from publications about COVID-19/SARS-CoV-2 that BOTH involve at least one author affiliated with a Dutch research institution AND openly share reusable data and/or code. After July 2023, we instead refer to the Dutch National COVID-19 metadata portal, where information about covid-related projects within the Netherlands can be found. Not all entries might openly share reusable data.

The lists of available data may not be exhaustive. If you find that a dataset is missing, or that the information related to it is incorrect, please get in touch with us
