This section includes a list of upcoming conferences, webinars, workshops, and training opportunities related to pandemic preparedness and infectious disease. All events are open to Swedish researchers. If you know of an appropriate event that is not listed here, please suggest it using the form below. All times are given in Central European Time (CET/CEST). Questions about individual events should be directed to the organisers of that event.

Events related to research data management in general are instead listed on the [SciLifeLab Data Platform](https://data.scilifelab.se/events/).
